        Assignment Briefs

        Summative Assessment

        1. Presentation (10%)

          Deadline: Tuesday 15 and Friday 18 January 2022 (in-class).

        2. Portfolio (70%)

          Deadline: Monday, 18 April 2022 before 16:00.

        3. Portfolio Documentation (20%)

          Deadline: Monday, 18 April 2022 before 16:00.

        Assessment Criteria

        • Understanding of theoretical, historical, critical and technical aspects of computation, creativity and computer music.
        • Creativity and originality in technical and/or creative work.
        • Sophistication in the understanding and application of human-machine co-creativity concepts.
        • Well presented ideas, documentation and code.
        • Critical analysis (concepts, algorithms, code, sound, interaction, etc.).
        • Lateral thinking (concepts, algorithms, code, sound, interaction, etc.).
        • Proficiency using and combining music technologies and techniques.
        • Evidence of research including suitable citations and bibliography.

        In your project you should respond to the following key points that will be evaluated in your Presentation, Portfolio and Portfolio Documentation:

        • Concept/idea of project
        • Research
        • Realisation of creative, technical and/or research outputs
        • Programming skills
        • Project management and collaboration
        • Presentation
        • Critical evaluation
